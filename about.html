What are algorithms?

algorithm. noun. a procedure for solving a mathematical problem (as of finding the greatest common divisor) in a finite number of steps that frequently involves repetition of an operation broadly : a step-by-step procedure for solving a problem or accomplishing some end.

Essentially, an algorithm is a sequence of instructions that is used to solve a very specific task. Search engines are the most prevalent examples of algorithms today.

Other examples of algorithms are cooking recipies, rubix cube solving formulas, facial recognition, basic information sorters, and more.
